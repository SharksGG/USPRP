About Us

We are a "roleplay" style simulation of the United States government at the federal, state, and local levels. We offer:

  1. Federal
    • Congress with both chambers and room for inter-chamber debates
    • All 15 current Executive Departments, POTUS, VPOTUS, President Pro Tempore
    • Supreme Court
  2. State & Local
    • 10 distinct regions to be governed including a region for U.S. territories (with more to come as the playerbase grows)
    • Any city in the 50 states, the District of Columbia, and U.S. territories are available for mayors if not taken already

    3. Not a politician?All citizens get:

    • Ability to start a political party, news outlet, or business
    • Ability to file a patent
    • Ability to privately call someone via the landline channel (POTUS: Use the Presidential landline for POTUS-Gov calls)
    • Ability to vote in mayoral, governor, congressional, or presidential elections
